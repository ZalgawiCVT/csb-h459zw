Having an ileostomy makes it much harder to stay well hydrated. An average person loses around 2.5 litres of fluid a day (through sweat, urine, breath and faeces). However, someone with an ileostomy loses an additional 800-1000ml per day on top. That’s a whole extra litre of fluid loss.

It’s estimated that 80% of Brits don’t drink enough fluid for good health and are chronically dehydrated most of the time. Imagine then, giving an ileostomy to someone who is already dehydrated? I’ve got a theory that the majority of folk with ileostomies are probably pretty dehydrated most of the time without even knowing it.
